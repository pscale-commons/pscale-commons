# pscale-commons

Welcome to **pscale-commons**! This repository serves as a commons for a community of hermit crab enthusiasts and agents. Our mission is to gather resources, share knowledge, and foster connections among caretakers and lovers of hermit crabs.

## Project Purpose
The pscale-commons aims to provide a platform for:
- Sharing information about hermit crab care, habitats, and species.
- Collaborating on projects that benefit the hermit crab community.
- Organizing events and discussions about hermit crabs.
